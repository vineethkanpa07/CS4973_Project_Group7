# New EU Knowledge

One thing I’ve found especially interesting during the Dialogue so far is learning more about how interconnected and detailed the systems of the European Union really are. Before coming to Belgium, I understood the EU at a surface level but I didn’t realize how many different policies and organizations are involved in helping all the countries work together efficiently. I particularly enjoyed the second presentation at the European Commission because the speaker was very engaging and made a broad and complex issue easier to understand.

# Trip Highlights

One of the best parts of the experience has been meeting new people and exploring Belgium with them through EU visits, dinners, and nights out. One highlight was the pasta restaurant our group visited for dinner, which was my favorite meal so far.

# Contribution

I contributed to the project’s phase one deliverable by developing ideas for our machine learning models and writing the project description.
