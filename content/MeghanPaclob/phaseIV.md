## Phase IV Deliverable Contribution
For the past week I've been working primarily on the student pages of the web app. After the presentation with Seamus I made a lot of changes to the UI based on the received feedback. A couple days later after the presentation with the professors, I made more drastic changes and a lot of updates to the web app. Primarily, changes to the flow and feature implementation of the student persona as well cleaning or revising mock data and inserting data into empty tables. After user testing, I updated the student persona again in things like the look of the dashboard, preventing multiple quiz submissions, and ensuring the functionality of the simulation.

## Week 4

As for food, I really liked the malatang place and was honestly really surprised it even existed here in Leuven. I also tried the Japanese cheesecake from Uncle Puffy and that was really good too. Despite how much I have enjoyed my time on the Dialogue, I'm excited to go back to Boston and see my friends again.
