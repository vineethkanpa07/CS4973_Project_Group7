# First Week in Leuven:

I have found the most interesting visit so far to be the European Commission. Learning about how the initiatives of the EU tie directly into everyday lives all over the world is intriguing to me. For example, I enjoyed learning about the motives behind company policies regardig individual state restrictions, and how laws in states like Texas influence business decisions to reach a larger audience. I was also interested in the different methods used for proper age verification rather than simply requesting the users’ information. Using credit card information or facial age estimation are both efficient ways to ensure children are unable to obtain access to adult content. Overall, I love understanding the reasoning behind laws today, and the technology behind these policies.
