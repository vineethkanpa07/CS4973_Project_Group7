## Individual Contributions

There was a lot of back and forth regarding the REST API Matrix, as originally we framed the DDL around the different personas. After realizing it made more sense to organize by user, I went back and re-routed the segments to ensure everything was more streamlined. This provided a basis for the routing, which I managed. Working on the routes was extremely time-consuming as I was learning and developing the structure of the app as I went. I also had to make minor edits to other files to ensure the template variables aligned with our own projects. This includes ensuring "eutopia_db" was implemented in every file, and that we all kept the same naming conventions for everything. 

An issue I kept running into was the "api" container was not running. This caused hours of delay as I was unable to figure out what was making this not run. Then, I discovered that the routes file was located in /api, not /api/backend which is where it was called. After solving this issue, everything ran smoothly, and the route testing ran flawlessly.
